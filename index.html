Final paper: my portfolio of experiences

by Francesco Silvani, III A, CLIL Address, 2020-2021

Introduction

As soon as I arrived in Middle School I did not feel ready for the adventure that awaited me.
Although it was not easy, I am satisfied with my path.
Since March 2020, covid has disrupted my daily life and has given me a lot of time to reflect on myself.
During the long periods of lockdown I learned the true value of friendship, because during that time only friends with a stronger bond remained.
In the field of socialization, my growth was mainly linear: In the sixth grade I felt very anonymous and I didn't have many friends.
At the beginning of the second, when I started to have more confidence, the covid and the restrictions related to it stopped this social development.
The eighth grade was the most profitable year for my socialization. In fact, I have recently become familiar with many more companions.
Over the years I have developed many interests, such as history, technology, science.
Precisely for this reason I chose a scientific secondary school, opting for a high school because it would have opened many more doors for me for the future.
Over the years I have become more autonomous, so not only the rules of the institute but also those of the anticovid, have helped me to become a responsible and aware citizen.
The initial choice of attending clil, studying geography and science in English proved to be valid for my personal development.
In conclusion, as mentioned, even if I encountered various difficulties during the year, especially for the German, I am definitely satisfied with my path during these three years
